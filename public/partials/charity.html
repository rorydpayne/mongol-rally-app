Charity

We are using the rally to help us raise money for 5 different charities: Cool Earth, Mind, Dementia UK, British Heart Foundation and Rowcroft Hospice. We’ve chosen these charities because we believe the work they do is incredible. We aim to raise £2,000 per charity and hope to run multiple fundraisers over the coming months – 100% of these fundraisers will go directly to the charities.

Cool Earth

Cool Earth is a UK-based international NGO that protects endangered rainforests in order to combat global warming, protect ecosystems and to provide employment for local people. It works alongside indigenous villages to halt rainforest destruction. All Cool Earth partnerships are community owned and led.

Learn More Donate Mind

Mind offers information and advice to people with mental health problems, and lobbies government and local authorities on their behalf. It also works to raise public awareness and understanding of issues relating to mental health.

Learn More Donate Dementia UK

Dementia UK provides support and guidance to those suffering from dementia – it helps them to live more positively and face the challenges it brings. Reuben's connection to this charity is particularly personal as his grandparents have suffered from this disease.

British Heart Foundation

The British Heart Foundation funds research, education, care and awareness campaigns aimed to prevent heart diseases in humans. Coronary heart disease is the UK’s biggest killer and Pete’s granddad was sadly lost to a heart attack.

Learn More Donate Rowcroft Hospice

Rowcroft Hospice is a wonderful Devon charity that provides care for 2,200 people each year. Jeremy's mum spent her final few weeks with Rowcroft - they're a fantastic organisation that make a massive difference to so many.
