Team

Meet the lads and lady!

Jeremy

A wildling reared in the feral depths of Dartmoor, Jeremy will be giving his alfresco lifestyle a boost up to full scale primitivism on the dusty track to Mongolia. A man whose adventurous childhood has been credited for inspiring Disney’s latest Mowgli, he’ll be bringing the steely grit of Clint Eastwood and the charming practicality of Bear Grylls to our roaming ramble. Either found cycling up and down the UK, or motorbiking his way round on some rudimentary two wheeler, he's not one for walking - his feet never touch the ground. As our resident first-aider, mechanic and official mountain man (yes, really), Jeremy will be acting as the team’s metaphorical bubble-wrap.
Katlyn

A lady truly worthy of the title Rambler, Katyln’s been a London native for the past 6 years, but has spent most of that time drifting round the world, rolling from one adventure to the next. Although she has the unenviable position of being the only girl in the gang, she’ll be whipping the chaps into an orderly procession across the globe, whilst sprinkling a certain hipster style onto our rag-tag group. Bringing her East end street smarts to the downtown alleys of Istanbul and beyond, Katlyn’s the brains of the band who’ll be leading the Mongolian charge from the front of the pack.
Pete

Product Manager by day (we don’t know what that is either), sleepy dreamer by night, Pete will be bringing misguided optimism and a startlingly pensive stare to the team. Guilty of brandishing amateur works of poetry at the unsuspecting throngs of London’s pubs, and fond of lurking in the backdrop of the occasional feature film, this wannabe artiste will spend most of his time gazing wistfully into the panoramic vistas of the East. Bringing no mechanical know-how or any practical skills of note, he’s banking on plain sailing from here to Mongolia. (Pete dislikes talking about himself in the third person, but has been known to make exceptions).
Reuben

With more names than a bigamous royal, Reuben’s sprawling identity will promise plenty of delayed border crossings and countless raised eyebrows in the countries to come. Despite his provision of bureaucratic turmoil, Reuben will be keeping the team chirpy and chipper with his indestructible cheeriness. Known for his famously unusual (and slightly suspicious) over-indulgence in hats, this rambunctious fellow will be making sure we're all well rested on the road through his adamant insistence to sleep in any and all places. Although well meaning in his organisation, he’s been guilty of a slightly fumbling attitude towards valuables in the past, so we’ve quietly cut key copies for the Yaris...just in case.
Rory

Software developer and aspiring mechanic, Rory will be bolstering spirits along the trail with his unending arsenal of woeful dad-jokes. A man chiselled from the lowlands of Scotland, you can often spy him traipsing through London’s winter in nothing more than a pair of flip flops. Despite his criminal footwear and dismissal of all coat-wearers as ‘jessies’, Rory's outdoorsy aspirations haven't yet got him further than Regent’s park. Once a professional Muay Thai boxer, he’ll be keeping us safe from highwaymen and gypsies, along with satisfying the hunger of the troop with his plentiful supply of Twixes.

And of course, the wondrous trio of mechanical marvels:

Bikes

Rory and Jeremy will be taking these two 1993 Yamaha TT250Rs as their steeds for the road ahead. At a blistering max speed of 60-ish mph, there’ll be less majestic cantering and more coughing, spluttering and stalling from this old pair of Japanese warriors. With the aim of getting more gizmos and gadgets than a pimp my ride gone wrong, the guys are currently acquiring any mods and accessories they can muster to help improve the chances of this dirt bike duo. To top it off, Rory’s promised a wheelie in each country, so the tough little thumpers have circus acrobatics added to their list of duties too...thanks Rory.

Car

Forget Land Rovers, BMWs and Jeeps, the mighty Toyota Yaris is the car you want for a 10,000 mile overland journey. The unbridled power of its 1L engine will be hurtling Katyln, Reuben and Pete from Goodwood to Ulan Ude with ease...or perhaps not. The trio are (desperately) scrabbling around for ways to make their beloved little Yaris slightly more suitable for the bumps and scrapes of what’s to come. This scrappy little chappie will be punching well above its weight along the dusty desert tracks and high mountain passes, but fear not, Pete’s mum has a Yaris and he’s driven it round Oxfordshire, so we reckon the gang’s golden.
