Team

Meet the lads and lady!

Jeremy

After a borderline feral upbringing in the depths of Dartmoor, surviving predominantly on a diet of mud and 10W40, Jeremy brings an element of the wild to our ranks. Living on the fringes of our technologically advanced society, this Devon wild-man is only truly at peace riding anything two-wheeled aimlessly around the most sparsely populated corners of the country. Indeed, Jeremy's two favourite human inventions are the bicycle and the internal combustion engine and is especially fond of the two in combination.

Katlyn

Hi! I'm Katlyn and I'm 24 years old. I have been living in London for almost 6 years but since I turned 18 I have been travelling all across the world. I joined my friends on the Mongol Rally as it gave me the opportunity to visit some super interesting and exotic countries I wouldn't visit otherwise, all whilst learning some mechanics and how to survive in a Yaris for 2 months! I am very excited about the opportunity to throw myself into this adventure and push myself and the Yaris to the limit!

Pete

Product Manager by day (we don’t know what that is either), sleepy dreamer by night, Pete will be bringing misguided optimism and a startlingly pensive stare to the team. Guilty of brandishing amateur works of poetry at the unsuspecting throngs of London’s pubs, and fond of lurking in the backdrop of the occasional feature film, this wannabe artiste will spend most of his time gazing wistfully into the panoramic vistas of the East. Bringing no mechanical know-how or any practical skills of note, he’s banking on plain sailing from here to Mongolia. (Pete dislikes talking about himself in the third person, but has been known to make exceptions).

Reuben

Reuben Thomas Daniel Lawrence Kettle Aiers has a name which is about as long as the Mongol Rally. Reuben brings several unique attributes to the team including an array of stylish headwear, and a strong tendency to misplace important items. We are delighted to have this fun-loving Londoner come Bristolian along for the ride as he has an incredible ability to keep everyone smiling no matter how tough the situation gets.
